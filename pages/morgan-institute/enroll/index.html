Your Teacher

Vanessa Morgan, L.Ac.

Diplomate of Homeopathy · Diplomate of Acupuncture · Licensed Acupuncturist

Vanessa began studying herbal medicine at seventeen with a Mohawk herbalist on Vancouver Island. Over the next fifty years, she trained in Chinese medicine in Hawaii and China, studied biological medicine in Germany, practiced and taught in London for twelve years, and developed her own integrated perspective on Samuel Hahnemann's Miasm Theory.

She has lectured in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Ireland, and Germany. Her work with homeopathic snake venom remedies — particularly for long-haul COVID and vaccine injury — has helped patients worldwide.

The Morgan Institute is the culmination of fifty years of integrated clinical wisdom.
