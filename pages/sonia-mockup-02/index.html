Mockup 02 · Clean Conversion Homepage

Turn your expertise into an offer people actually buy.

This version is cleaner, calmer, and more conversion-first. It is built for clarity over theatrics. The homepage explains what Sonia does, who it is for, what changes, and what the next step is — without asking the visitor to decode the business.

For: established experts, executives, founders, and professionals who need sharper positioning and a premium offer.
Problem: they have real expertise, but it is not packaged into something that sells clearly.
Outcome: a stronger offer, clearer message, and sales path that does not rely on content chaos.

Why this one

Best if you want more trust and less noise.

This concept is less dramatic than the authority-speaker version and may convert better if the current audience needs easier orientation before they book.

What it emphasizes

Offer clarity, audience fit, clean messaging, and a more straightforward path from curiosity to conversation.

Best use case

If SoniaGomez.net is meant to be the stable central homepage rather than a campaign page, this is probably the safer direction.

Strategic difference

What this version would fix on the current site

This version does not try to impress first. It tries to orient first. That usually improves conversion when the audience is serious, experienced, and not looking for guru theatrics.

What to avoid

  • too much broad inspiration
  • unclear “what exactly do you do?” moments
  • too many competing offers at once
  • brand personality before business clarity

What this adds

  • clean fit qualification
  • clear offer-first positioning
  • stronger headline discipline
  • single primary CTA
Recommended structure

How I would build the full homepage from here

1. Hero

Clear promise, clear audience, one CTA.

2. Who it’s for

Explain who Sonia helps and, importantly, who this is not for.

3. Why offers come first

Teach the philosophy that the offer matters more than generic brand-building.

4. Proof

Add outcomes, speaking wins, client examples, and sharper credibility blocks.

5. Ways to work together

Explain the path into the ecosystem instead of presenting a pile of options.

6. Fit call CTA

End with a calm, direct application flow.

Recommendation

If this were my call, I’d combine both directions.

Use the authority and premium feel of Mockup 01, but keep the cleaner conversion structure of Mockup 02. That would likely be the strongest homepage for what you have right now.

  • Mockup 01 wins on emotional authority and premium feel
  • Mockup 02 wins on clarity and lower-friction conversion
  • The best live version is probably a hybrid, not a pure winner-take-all choice
